Hi, my name is Camille and I have some advice for you!

Keep moving forward!

Try not to get dicouraged or overly frustrated, especially if you are coming back for a second career. It's hard to get started in a totally new subject. Keep coming back, working on the lessons, and studying.

Don't expect to understand everything right away. It's really easy to think you should understand all of it after one shot/one read-through becuase you are an adult and maybe even have a whole career behind you. But, remember how long it took you to become an expert in your field and keep that in mind if you are struggling.

Keep coming back

It might take more than one attempt to fully understand something or "get it right." And you migth even have to take a break for a while which is fine. I started this during the pandemic (summer of 2020) hoping to be done by December of that year. It's spring of 2022 now. So..you can se how well that worked out. I started again and this time around it's a little easier.
