Hi, my name is Adrienne and I have some advice for you!

Consistency is your friend!

Like anything in life, consistency is key to building skills.

Consistency requires dedication and intentionality. It allows us to learn through repetition. Dedication to consistently practicing pays off by aquired skills.

Perserverence

Even if you have no idea whether you're doing something right, perservering will eventually pay off because you'll figure it out in time. Trust in the process and perservere until you figure it out.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You'll get there! Just be patient!
