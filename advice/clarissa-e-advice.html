Hi, my name is Clarissa and I have some advice for you!

Believe in yourself.

Jumping into somthing new may be easy, but sticking to it is the challenge. Be your #1 supporter through the journey! There may be lots of doubt along the way, but showing up for yourself makes all the difference.

Trust the process.

We all learn new things in different ways and at different rates. That doesn't make anyone's approach or pace better or worse. The important thing is to keep going.

Refresh.

Let yourself breathe and step away. Sometimes, you may not be feeling it or you may be mentally overwhelmed with so much new information. Taking a break is not a bad thing. You need it!
