Self Encouragement

The best advice I can give to someone just beginning is DON'T PANIC!

I know it can seem overwhelming and frustrating but stick with it! No one knows a new skill the first time he try it. It's important to keep your chin up and push through the tough lessons. In the end it's so worth it. Not everyone can say the speak computer, right?!

Support system

The next biggest key to success is to have a good support system. Family, friends, classmates, instructors, anyone you can rely on! It makes a huge difference in preformance when you have your own cheering squad. Personally, it's my husband and three kids! They're the reason I am pursuing this career path.
