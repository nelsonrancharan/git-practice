Hi, my name is Jesica and I have some advice for you!

I'm learning a new language, and that takes time and practice!

I find it really helpful to recognize the parallel between learning new languages and learning to code. It takes time, and it feels so uncomfortable at first! You have to look up anything unfamiliar quite a few times before it really sticks, and just like with learning a language like Spanish, it takes hands on practice - during which it's A-O-K to not be communicating perfectly!

Learning from mistakes is what will make the right way forward really strick a bit extra!

Chip away a little at a time, and big change will come along with time.

Just like you can't learn a foreign language overnight, you certainly can't learn how to code overnight either! Patience is your best friend, and recognizing those small daily, weekly, and monthly milestones will make all the difference when it comes to keeping yourself motivated and inspired to keep pushing forward.
