Don't be embarrassed if you don't know yet

When you start learning in a new domain, there will be a lot of minor details that people with extensive knowledge in that domain already have and take for granted.

There's no need to be embarrassed if you are still learning. A curious attitude will serve you well on your journey of learning to code.
