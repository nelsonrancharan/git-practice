Hi, my name is Amanda and I have some advice to offer!

Remeber your Why

Your WHY will keep you going when you get frustrated and want to quit. WHY are you learning to code? WHY did you make this career change into tech? WHY exactly are you doing this?

There will be times when things get confusing and frustrated. In these times you will be tempted to quit, but don't! Remember your WHY, and give yourself a break, take a hot bath, anything to help you recharged and clear your mind. Then continue on, things always look better in the morning!

Different paces

You might be a natural when learning HTML & CSSbut feel lost with Git/GitHub. Expect that somethings will come easier than others. Embrace it! and keep going.

Be kind to yourself

Learning something new can be confusing and challenging. You are going to make mistakes and do things wrong, which can be very frustrating. However, this is all part of the learning process! So have patience and most importantly, be kind to yourself and allow yourself to make mistakes.

Reach out!

Do your best, try to solve your problems, but if you are at a dead end, don't go it alone, reach out for help. You will be surprise how many developers are willing to help you in your journey.

Finally Remember - YOU GOT THIS!!!
